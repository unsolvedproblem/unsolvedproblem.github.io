Hackerrank 레벨업하기  
문제   
[Extra Long Factorials](https://www.hackerrank.com/challenges/extra-long-factorials/problem)

~~~
def extraLongFactorials(n):
    a = 1
    for i in range(1,n + 1):
        a = a * i
    print (a)
~~~
