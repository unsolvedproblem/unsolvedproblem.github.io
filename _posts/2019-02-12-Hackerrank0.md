문제   
[Abstract Classes](https://www.hackerrank.com/challenges/30-abstract-classes/problem)

~~~
class MyBook(Book):
    def __init__(self, title, author, price):
        self.title = title
        self.author = author
        self.price = price

    def display(self):
        print("Title:", self.title)
        print("Author:", self.author)
        print("Price:", self.price)
~~~
